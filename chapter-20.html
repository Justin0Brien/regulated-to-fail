The "Exempt" Status Trap

Normal Company

1. Buys IT Service (£100 + £20 VAT) Pay £120
2. Charges VAT on Sales Yes
3. Reclaims Input VAT? YES (Net Cost £100)
THE LEAK

University

1. Buys IT Service (£100 + £20 VAT) Pay £120
2. Charges VAT on Tuition? NO (Exempt)
3. Reclaims Input VAT? NO (Net Cost £120)

"Exempt means you don't charge it, but you also can't claim it back. The VAT sticks."

Why not use the Exemption? (The CSE Maze)

Cost Sharing Exemption

"Shared services at cost are exempt!"

Must be a separate legal entity.
Strict Cost-Only: No profit allowed. Hard to invest/grow.
Directly Necessary: Non-qualifying use disqualifies it.
UK Only: Cannot easily include int'l partners.

"The exemption exists, but the compliance cost and legal risk often outweigh the saving."
