Why the Bargain Broke

Policy Risk

Migration policy became volatile. Changes to dependant rules removed the stability required for long-term planning.

Correlation

Risks are no longer diversified. Currency shocks and geopolitics now move multiple markets in the same direction.

Visibility

The "quiet workaround" became public. International income is now seen as a policy target for caps or levies.

Over-Reliance

Every institution chased the same surplus, increasing acquisition costs just as the market tightened.
