Why You Cannot Simply "Cut Staff"

The structural reality of cost reduction.

  • Non-Pay is Stubborn: Firing staff does not lower energy bills, software licences, or compliance fees.
  • Fixed Costs Remain: Security, utilities, and debt service must be paid regardless of headcount.
  • The Spiral: Cutting academic staff damages the product -> recruitment falls -> revenue drops further.
