The Core Problem: Speed Mismatch

Institutional Commitments

Slow to change. Includes long-term staff contracts, Victorian buildings requiring heating, pension obligations, and "teach-out" promises to students.

Financial Reality

Fast deterioration. Driven by sharp drops in international enrolment, inflation, and immediate cash-flow gaps.

"A fire is what happens when a system that was designed for incremental adjustment discovers that it needs structural change."
