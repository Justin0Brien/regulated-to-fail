The Missing Safety Net

Higher Education is the exception.

Further Education (FE) PROTECTED

Has a Special Administration Regime (SAR). Administrator must prioritise learner continuity over creditors.

Higher Education (HE) EXPOSED

No SAR. Standard corporate insolvency applies. Students are unsecured creditors.

"The state has solved this for colleges, but not for universities."
