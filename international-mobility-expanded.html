Global International Student Movements (Expanded)

Visualising the flow of students from major origin countries to global study hubs. This chart aggregates the "European Union" and includes key Asian destinations (Japan, South Korea).

Sources: USA: IIE Open Doors (2023/24); UK: HESA (2022/23); Australia: Dept. of Education (2023); Japan: JASSO (2024); Germany: DAAD (2024); France: Campus France (2024).

Note: "European Union" (Dest) aggregates major EU hosts like Germany and France. "EU Students" (Source) represents EU nationals studying abroad. "North Africa" combines Morocco and Algeria.
