The Necessary Shift

The Subsidy Trap (Current)

  • • Subsidy is automatic and unmeasured.
  • • "Grants pay for research" is the accepted narrative.
  • • Deficit is hidden in general overheads.
  • • Strategy relies on "Hope" that surpluses continue.

Strategic Governance (Future)

  • • Subsidy is explicit and capped.
  • • fEC gap is treated as an "Investment Decision".
  • • Cross-subsidy source (Int'l Fees) is stress-tested.
  • • Research is managed as a portfolio, not a monolith.
