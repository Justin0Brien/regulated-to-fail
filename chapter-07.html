The Hidden "Undertow"

Research volume drags in fixed costs that are hard to cut.

  • Contracts & IP Teams: Specialist staff required.
  • Compliance: Ethics, security, and data protection.
  • Facilities: High-cost labs and equipment maintenance.
"Research was pursued... as a strategic identity marker in a quasi-market. Over time, that turned into an operating model where research intensity becomes a fixed characteristic... even when the funding model treats it as a variable cost centre."
