Global International Student Movements

Visualising the flow of students from top origin countries to the "Big Four" study destinations. Data sourced from 2022/23 and 2023/24 academic reports.

Sources: USA: IIE Open Doors (2023/24); UK: HESA (2022/23); Australia: Dept. of Education (2023); Canada: CBIE/IRCC (2023).

Note: Data represents total enrolled students (stock) for the respective periods. "Canada" as a destination refers to inbound international students; "Canadian Students" refers to outbound nationals studying in the USA.
